ABOUT US

Providing Quality and Innovative Products with Strong Sustainability Value

Through the #LoveUGlowLoveEarth campaign, we have realized various massive efforts in supporting sustainable movements. Therefore, #MulaiDariMejaRias is here to invite Sahabat U Glow to be able to contribute to sustainability starting from themselves.

Some examples that we echoes such as U Glow who tirelessly educates Sahabat U Glow to use skincare that is personalized according to the needs of each skin so that it creates a "mindful skincare use", avoids the use of microplastics in our products, and facilitates Sahabat Avo to return empty packaging through a digital takeback program.

Our Principles: People, Planet, and Profit

Planet

Product ingredients are made with the sustainability of nature in mind

Using reusable packaging

Initiating beauty empties digital takeback program with Waste4Change and

Collaborating with environmental conservation organization like BOS Fondation and Lindungi Hutan

People

Developing community through RUBI to empower women in a sustainable manner

Worked together with Indonesian community such as Torajamelo socio-brand who supports the development of women's lives in Mamasa, Lembata and Adonara

Collaborating with non-profit organization like PUKA, local businesses that focus on crafting while providing creative media for the education of children with special needs

Profit

Fair trade system with our upstream to downstream partners. We make this effort to also make products at fair prices so that all sustainable activities that we carry out can also run sustainably
